Welcome

Hi, I'm Andy Brooks. Welcome to my homepage.


I'm a graduate mathematician from Bath University with

more than 35 years' experience of teaching and tutoring

maths in the northeast of England. I have worked in 5 local

secondary schools, including Stokesley, Nunthorpe, Thirsk

and Northallerton. I have been a Chief Examiner, and I have

had responsibility for writing examination papers at both

GCSE and A level. I was also a member of the AQA examiner

team that developed the current new A level Mathematics and

Further Mathematics specifications, which were first examined

in 2019.

A Level Maths Tuition


If you're studying maths at A level and looking for some

extra support, then let's have a conversation about how

Contact Me

Here are just a few ways in which I can make a difference:
  • I can re-explain a topic that you've found hard, then choose sensible questions for you to try, to build your confidence and ability
  • I can give you help with answering difficult problems, by explaining the key facts and methods you need to use
  • I can deconstruct exam questions, with hints and tips, so that you can gain confidence in knowing how to begin (and complete!) your answer
  • I can help you to develop your maths memory, so that you build the maths knowledge that you need to have at your fingertips
  • I can guide you to structure your learning, so that you increase your skills and understanding without increasing stress
  • I can help you to navigate your way through the many past exam papers, so that you focus on the right questions at the right time
